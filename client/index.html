About Us

Cargo is a comprehensive route management system designed to streamline the logistics and transportation operations of businesses. Leveraging advanced technologies such as GPS tracking and real-time mapping, Cargo enables efficient planning, tracking, and optimization of delivery routes.

  • Fast and reliable transportation services
  • Secure handling of valuable cargo
  • Efficient customs clearance solutions

Services

Freight Forwarding Services

Freight forwarding services encompass the organization and coordination of shipments from point of origin to destination. This involves negotiating with carriers, arranging transportation, and managing documentation to ensure smooth transit of goods.

Warehousing and Distribution

Warehousing and distribution services involve the storage, handling, and distribution of goods. Warehouses serve as hubs for inventory management, order fulfillment, and value-added services like packaging and labeling.

Customs Clearance and Compliance

Customs clearance and compliance services ensure that shipments adhere to all regulatory requirements for international trade. This involves managing import/export documentation, tariff classification, and facilitating clearance through customs authorities.

Logistics Management

Logistics management services encompass the planning, coordination, and optimization of transportation and inventory activities. This includes route planning, carrier selection, tracking and tracing of shipments, and overall supply chain optimization to maximize efficiency and minimize costs.
